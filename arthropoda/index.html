Each segment develops two thicker patches on the upper sides.  It’s harder to kill and eat you, but you do move slower.  In time, the patches of thicker armour extend further and further.

Your simple eyes develop further, by accidental repetition which at first gives you a slightly better view of the world, into larger multiple eyes containing hundreds of simple eyes - which gives you a much improved view on the world. These are called compound eyes.  In a compound eye, the elongated, tiny, simple eyes are called ommatidia.

There are two patterns which are efficient for growing compound eyes: hexagonal and square.  Square is better when you are very small and have very few ommatidia, and hexagonal is better when you are big and have many.  All other arrangements are less effective.
